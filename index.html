CMGTwitch

What is CMGTwitch?

CMGTwitch is a platform for students to find their past, current and future lectures. You can also find your lecture based on topic/subject. At last you have the posibility to make a ticket to get your questions answered.

How does the ticketsystem work?

During live lectures you can make a ticket. In this ticket you can leave your question. During the live lecture teachers and students are able to answer your question. You can read the answers in the ticket tab in the menu. You can see your open tickets without answers and answered questions. You can do this by opening the ticket. You can see the answers and a way to archive your ticket when answered.
